---
title: "Flash Frothy"
layout: "flash"
description: "Install Frothy on a compatible ESP32 board from your browser."
---

Connect a compatible ESP32 over USB, choose the board, and flash its ready-made
firmware. The segmented update leaves Frothy's saved project storage intact.

After flashing, **Check Frothy** sends a small status request to confirm that
the board is reachable before you continue to the editor.
